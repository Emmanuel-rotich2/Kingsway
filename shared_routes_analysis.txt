   Deputy Head - Discipline and School Administrator. This may be
   intentional (driver broadcasts) or an error.

2. CLASS TEACHER & SUBJECT TEACHER ARE NEARLY IDENTICAL

   difference: Class Teacher has manage_assessments; Subject Teacher
   does not. Consider whether this distinction is still meaningful.

3. BOARDING_ROLL_CALL GOES TO TEACHERS
   Class Teacher and Subject Teacher have boarding_roll_call access,
   which seems unusual unless they serve as boarding duty staff.

4. DEPUTY HEAD - DISCIPLINE LACKS student_performance
   DH-Discipline manages discipline but cannot view student performance,
   which could be needed for context on discipline decisions.

5. DIRECTOR LACKS manage_students
   Director can see students via sidebar but has no role_routes entry

   only, creating an inconsistency.

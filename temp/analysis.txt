3. KENYAN PRIMARY SCHOOL ROLES ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------
Kenyan Primary School Roles and Functions:

System Administrator:
  Description: Technical system management, database, codebase
  Primary Functions:
    • System configuration and settings
    • User and role management
    • Database management
    • System monitoring and logs
    • Backup and security
    • Technical support

School Administrator:
  Description: Operational school management
  Primary Functions:
    • Daily operations oversight
    • Staff coordination
    • Student records management
    • Parent communication
    • Report generation
    • Policy implementation

Director:
  Description: Strategic leadership and financial oversight
  Primary Functions:
    • Financial reports review
    • Budget approvals
    • Strategic planning
    • Board reporting
    • Major decisions approval
    • Stakeholder relations

Headteacher:
  Description: Academic and administrative leadership
  Primary Functions:
    • Academic year planning
    • Curriculum oversight
    • Staff management
    • Student admissions (with deputies)
    • Parent-teacher meetings
    • School calendar management
    • Academic performance monitoring

Deputy Head - Academic:
  Description: Academic programs management
  Primary Functions:
    • Timetable creation
    • Exam scheduling and management
    • Student promotion decisions
    • Academic reports compilation
    • Teacher supervision
    • Curriculum implementation

Deputy Head - Discipline:
  Description: Student discipline and welfare
  Primary Functions:
    • Discipline management
    • Student admissions processing
    • Parent communication (discipline)
    • Suspension/expulsion recommendations
    • Guidance and counseling coordination

Class Teacher:
  Description: Class management and teaching
  Primary Functions:
    • Daily attendance marking
    • Class assessments
    • Results entry
    • Lesson plans creation
    • Parent communication (class)
    • Class reports
    • Student welfare monitoring

Subject Teacher:
  Description: Subject-specific teaching
  Primary Functions:
    • Subject teaching
    • Assessments and exams
    • Results entry (subject)
    • Lesson plans (subject)
    • Student performance tracking

Accountant:
  Description: Financial management
  Primary Functions:
    • Fee collection and tracking
    • Payment processing
    • Payroll management
    • Financial reports
    • Budgeting
    • Bank reconciliation
    • Supplier payments
    • P9 and payslip generation

Cateress:
  Description: Kitchen and food services management
  Primary Functions:
    • Menu planning
    • Food store inventory
    • Kitchen staff supervision
    • Meal preparation oversight
    • Food requisitions
    • Nutrition compliance

Boarding Master:
  Description: Boarding facility management
  Primary Functions:
    • Boarding student welfare
    • Health monitoring
    • Dormitory management
    • Night supervision
    • Parent communication (boarding)
    • Discipline (boarding)

Talent Development:
  Description: Sports, music, and co-curricular activities
  Primary Functions:
    • Sports programs management
    • Music and drama activities
    • Competitions organization
    • Talent identification
    • Activity scheduling
    • Equipment management

Driver:
  Description: School transport
  Primary Functions:
    • Student transport
    • Route management
    • Vehicle maintenance reporting
    • Trip logs

Chaplain:
  Description: Spiritual and pastoral care
  Primary Functions:
    • Spiritual guidance
    • Student counseling
    • Chapel services
    • Pastoral care
    • Moral education

Inventory Manager:
  Description: School uniforms and supplies store
  Primary Functions:
    • Uniform inventory
    • Stock management
    • Requisitions processing
    • Uniform sales/distribution
    • Stock valuation
